November 20 1998 22:01pm

The tapping noise continues.
It appears to be coming from the bathroom.
Your heart almost feels as if it is going to jump out of your chest as you tread slowly up the stairs towards the source of the noise.
*TAP* *TAP* *TAP*
It is definitely coming from the bathroom.
You hold in your breath as you stand outside the room.
Hastily you open the bathroom door.
With the help of your light source you scan the room.
...nothing is there.
Then you notice the noise again radiating from the window.
Sighing in relief,you realise it was just a branch from the tree outside hitting the window as the wind surges.
You decide head back to the living room.
As you stroll back downstairs, you hear shuffling followed by the creak of floorboards behind you.
> Do you turn to face the noise?
OR
> Do you rush down the stairs?
