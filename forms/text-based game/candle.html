November 20 1998 21:41pm

You make your way through the darkness of the living room to the kitchen.
After living in the house all your life you found it more than easy to make your way to the kitchen in the black of the night.
You open the drawer next to the sink and find the tealights.
You feel around the same drawer for the matchbox.
Once you find it,you strike the match and light the candle placed on the counter-top.
Finally, some light.
As you turn to exit the kitchen you notice a dark figure pass the window.
It must be the leaves blowing in the wind, you say to yourself.
> You make your way back to the living room.
