Quicker than you have ever done, you speed down the stairs.
You decide to go back to the living room and hide.
But where do I hide?
You think to yourself.
The only place that you know for sure you can hide is underneath the table-cloth covered dinner table.
*creak*
The noise is getting closer.
Another creak rings in your ears…
It is definitely footsteps.
Your heart beats at a million mph as the steps come closer.
You cover your mouth trying to stop the sound of your panting from alerting whatever is making those footsteps.
And just then…

The footsteps stop abruptly at the entrance of the living room.
