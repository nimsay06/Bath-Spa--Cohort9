November 20 1998 21:41pm

You get yourself up from the carpet floor and walk towards the door of your basement.
The floorboards creek with every step taken, the noise of them jarring the constant rain hitting every window of the house.
You make your way down the basement stairs, its colder than usual.
To the right catch a glimpse of the breaker box as another strike of lightning hits.
You flick the main power switch but to no avail, the power is truly out and by the looks of things the whole street seems affected too.
You rummage around the boxes and find the emergency flashlight.
*click*
The flashlight switches on and your shine the light around the cold basement noticing that one of the windows that face street level is open.
Without thinking much about it, you shut it hard ensuring that the wind doesnt open it.
> You make your way back to the living room.
